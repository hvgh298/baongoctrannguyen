2024
Jerry in bathtub
Animatronic interactive toy
This project is inspired by an iconic scene from Tom and Jerry, where Jerry is taking a bath, and Tom opens the window. Jerry's immediate reaction is to scream and hit Tom with a brush. For this project, I experimented with an animatronic figure that interacts with the player. I implemented motors to create movement effects for Jerry's arm and tongue, which respond to signals from a limit switch when the player opens the window. Throughout this process, I learned how to rig the bones of the model in Blender and pose it for 3D printing. I used Blender to design Jerry, ensuring that he captures the playful spirit of the character. The purpose of this project is to create an interactive toy for children, providing them with a fun and engaging experience.
Credit: Ca Yen Linh
